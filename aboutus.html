About Us

About Us

Welcome to The Floating Fork! At The Floating Fork, we believe that food is not just about taste—it’s an experience. Nestled in the heart of Pune, our restaurant combines a passion for delicious cuisine with a cozy, welcoming ambiance. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a place to celebrate a special occasion, we promise to make every meal unforgettable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring people together over great food. We aim to create memorable dining experiences by serving fresh, flavorful dishes crafted with love and care. Your happiness is our top priority, and we strive to exceed your expectations with every visit.

Our Story

Started in 2007, The Floating Fork was born out of a love for culinary creativity and a dream to connect with our community. What began as a small eatery has grown into a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike. From classic comfort foods to innovative dishes, we take pride in offering a menu that has something for everyone.

What Makes Us Special?

  • Fresh Ingredients: We source the finest, freshest ingredients to ensure every dish bursts with flavor.
  • Authentic Recipes: Our menu blends traditional and contemporary recipes for a unique culinary journey.
  • Warm Atmosphere: From our friendly staff to our beautifully designed space, we want you to feel right at home.
  • Community Focus: We’re proud to be part of the Pune community, hosting events and supporting local causes.

Join Us!

We invite you to come and savor our delicious dishes, share moments with loved ones, and be part of our story. At The Floating Fork, every meal is a celebration of food, family, and friendship.
